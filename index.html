About AirSwap

AirSwap is an innovative eco-technology startup dedicated to combating air pollution through smart IoT devices and community-based incentives.

We transform regular household fans and AC units into intelligent air cleaners that monitor air quality and reward users with AirPoints for every bit of clean air they help produce.

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities to take action against air pollution while making sustainable living rewarding and engaging.
